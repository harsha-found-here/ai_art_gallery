  • What is AI generated Art??

    AI-generated art is artistic content created with the aid of artificial intelligence algorithms, such as neural networks like GANs and VAEs. These algorithms learn from vast datasets of existing art to autonomously produce new and unique pieces without direct human intervention in the creative process.   • How are the artworks created

    Artworks in the gallery are created using sophisticated neural network models like GANs and VAEs, learning from vast art datasets to generate diverse pieces. These algorithms analyze patterns from existing art to produce new, imaginative compositions showcased in the gallery.

  • what makes AI art unique ?

    AI art is unique for its collaboration between humans and algorithms, generating novel outputs autonomously. It blends human input with machine creativity, adapting over time. This efficient, scalable process explores unconventional patterns, offering interactive, dynamic experiences.

  • AI art authentic ?

    AI art authentic ? AI art authenticity varies, as it's created by algorithms trained on existing art. There's ongoing debate around its originality and how it aligns with traditional notions of authenticity in the art world.
